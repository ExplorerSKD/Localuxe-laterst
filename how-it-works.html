Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using Localuxe

How accurate are the cost estimates?

Our AI analyzes current pricing data from multiple sources to provide realistic cost estimates. However, actual costs may vary based on season, specific venues, and personal spending habits.

Can I customize the generated itinerary?

Currently, you can influence the itinerary by selecting different interests and budget levels. Future updates will include full customization features allowing you to modify specific activities and timings.

Does Localuxe work for all cities worldwide?

Our AI has comprehensive data for major tourist destinations worldwide. For smaller or less documented locations, the recommendations might be more general but still helpful for planning.

Is my personal information stored or shared?

We only collect the information you provide in the planning form to generate your itinerary. We don't store personal data or share information with third parties.
